<h3 id="lyrics">Lyrics</h3>

That feeling when you start missing your friends during the last few days at your college. Our try to capture that unforgettable moment in everyone's life!

<div class="code-head">original<span>lyrics</span></div>

```
  #--------
  # intro
  #--------
  Engiruntho naam vanthom
  Vinnai thodave naam inainthom
  Kallori enbathu nammidam neengatha uyirada
  Natpin ulagilae
  Paasathin valiyilae
  Theeratha anbilae
  Nanainthom kallori moochile

  #---------
  # pallavi
  #---------
  Nanbane nanbane
  Ithu ondrum mudivillai thodakkamada
  Ninaivugal thodaruthe
  Marupadi vaalanum unnudane

  #--------
  # chorus
  #--------
  Kalvi payilum kaalathil kaneer artham puriyavillai
  Indru purinthathu namathu natpin aalam athu therigindrathu
  Unnai pirintha nodiyinile Enthan ulagam irutiduthe
  Kangal moodinaal unudan vaaltha naatkal ennai kolgindrathe

  #----------
  # charanam
  #----------
  Nanban oruvan irunthaal pothum
  Vaalvil ethuvum athisayamaagum
  Vetri kumiyum inbangal perugum
  Magilchi kadalil mithakkum nenjam
  Natpu ennum manthira sollall
  ingu naangal inainthome
  4 varudam mudintha pinbu
  Piriya mattum vendame
  Bayamariyaa paruvam ithuve
  vidha vidha kanavugal seithu mudipom
  Jathi mathathirku arthamillai
  Paagupaade ingu illai

  #--------
  # chorus
  #--------
  Maru jenmam endrirunthaal
  Kallori mannaga pirakka vendum
  En uyir vaalum kaalathai
  Ingu matume selavidavendum
  Pirivai thaangum sakthi illai
  nanbargal eppothum thunai vara vendum
  Vaalvil ethuvum nilai illaya
  Bathil sollu iraiva engalukku
```
